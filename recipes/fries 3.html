Fries

French fries, or simply fries.

Are batonnet or allumette-cut deep-fried potatoes of disputed origin from Belgium and France
They are usually prepared by cutting potatoes into even strips, drying them, and frying them

Ingridients

  • 5 big potatoes
  • 2 cups of flour
  • salt (to taste)
  • oil

Steps

  1. Clean, peel and cut the potatoes into strips. After cutting, take it to the freeze for 10 minutes
  2. Take it out of the freezer and dry it out
  3. Pass the potatoes through the flour and slowly trhow in hot oil
  4. Take it out of the oil, dry it and have fun
